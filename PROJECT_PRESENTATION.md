## Slide 2: Problem Statement
- **Fragmented Market:** Students struggle to find reliable accommodation near colleges.
- **Trust Issues:** Lack of verified listings leads to fraud and safety concerns.
- **Inefficient Process:** Manual booking, lack of transparency, and cumbersome paperwork.
- **Communication Gap:** Difficulty in coordinating with owners and getting quick answers.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution - Roomies
- **All-in-One Platform:** A centralized marketplace for students and property owners.
- **Verified Listings:** Strict KYC and property verification for safety.
- **Seamless Booking:** End-to-end digital booking flow with secure payments.
- **AI-Powered:** Smart search and concierge support using Gemini AI.

---

## Slide 4: Key Features (Student)
- **Smart Search:** Filter by college, price, amenities, and location.
- **Interactive Map:** View properties on a Google Map with street view.
- **AI Chatbot:** "Roomies Assistant" for instant queries and recommendations.
- **Secure Booking:** Book rooms online with Razorpay integration.
- **Digital Contracts:** E-sign rental agreements hassle-free.

---

## Slide 5: Key Features (Owner)
- **Property Management:** Easy listing management and occupancy tracking.
- **Tenant Verification:** Screen student profiles and verification status.
- **Automated Payments:** Track rent payments and dues.
- **Dashboard:** Analytics on views, bookings, and revenue.

---

## Slide 6: Technology Stack
- **Backend:** Python (Flask), SQLAlchemy (ORM).
- **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (Vanilla).
- **Database:** PostgreSQL (Production) / SQLite (Dev).
- **APIs & Services:**
  - **Google Maps API:** Geolocation and mapping.
  - **Razorpay:** Payment gateway.
  - **Google Gemini AI:** Chatbot and content generation.
  - **SMTP:** Email notifications.

---

## Slide 7: Safety & Verification
- **Identity Check:** Admin-verified student and owner profiles.
- **Badge System:** Trusted users receive a "Verified" badge.
- **AI Verification:** (Planned) Automated document scanning using OpenCV.
- **Email Notifications:** Instant alerts for approvals and bookings.

---

## Slide 8: Business Model
- **Booking Fee:** Small service fee on confirmed bookings.
- **Owner Subscription:** Premium features for property owners.
- **Featured Listings:** Promoted spots for higher visibility.
